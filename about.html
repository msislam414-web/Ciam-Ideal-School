About CIAM Ideal School

Providing Quality Education Since 2011

Our History

CIAM Ideal School was established in 2011 with the vision of providing quality education, moral values, discipline, and modern learning opportunities for students. Since its establishment, the school has expanded successfully and now operates through five branches across Dhaka. Thousands of students have benefited from our commitment to academic excellence and character development.

Chairman & Principal

MD Ibrahim Chowdhury

Chairman & Principal of CIAM Ideal School. Dedicated to building future generations through quality education, discipline, and moral values.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that develops students academically, morally, socially, and spiritually so that they become responsible citizens and future leaders.

Our Vision

To become one of the leading educational institutions in Bangladesh through excellence in teaching, learning, and character building.
